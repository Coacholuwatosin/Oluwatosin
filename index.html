Who I Am

I’m Oluwatosin Adegboye, a multi-disciplinary digital professional who builds modern business websites, seamless mobile applications, creates compelling visual designs, and mentors people in skill development, leadership, and personal growth.

2
How It Started

I started like most people, trying to figure out how to make money online without getting lost or overwhelmed. At the time, I was broke, frustrated, and unsure of what direction to take.

I tried different opportunities, made costly mistakes, and struggled to find something that truly worked online. I invested time in the wrong platforms, lost money, and faced challenges that left me confused and stuck.

3
The Turning Point

Everything began to change when I found a simple and practical system that made sense. I stopped guessing, started learning real affiliate marketing and design skills, and applied them the right way. That was when I began to see results.

After some years, I was introduced to a business program that completely transformed my mindset and direction. That experience pushed me further into learning and growth, leading me to study software development and sharpen my digital skills. Since then, I have built real experience working on projects, earning through freelancing, and growing a passive income business through network marketing.

4
My Mission Today

Today, I combine all of that to help people learn valuable digital skills, build income streams, and actually start earning online. I don’t believe in theory without results. Everything I teach is practical, tested, and designed to help you start, grow, and stay consistent.

I believe the internet is the greatest equaliser of our generation. With the right skills, guidance, and mindset, anyone’s story can change. My mission is to show people exactly how — through hands-on mentorship, practical teachings, and proven strategies that help you make real progress.

FAQ

Common questions
about FHG

What is FHG (Faith Heroic Generation)?

Faith Heroic Generation (FHG), also known as FHG Org Limited, is a Nigerian youth empowerment organisation that helps individuals build financial freedom through freelancing and network marketing. Founded in late 2015, FHG gives members access to multiple income streams and valuable digital skills. Since its founding, FHG has grown to over 50,000 members across Nigeria and beyond.

Is FHG free to join?

Yes, joining FHG is completely free. There are no registration or sign-up fees. Once you express interest, you will be invited to a free introductory session where you learn about the structure, what is expected, and the income opportunities available. All training resources and community tools are accessible at no cost.

How does earning work in FHG?

Earning in FHG is built around network marketing and team building. You grow by bringing people into the business, supporting them to do the same, and earning commissions as your team expands. The bigger and more active your team, the higher your income. Coach Oluwatosin will guide you step by step on how to build and grow your team effectively from day one.

How does Coach Oluwatosin support new FHG members?

Coach Oluwatosin provides personal one-on-one guidance from the moment you join, an active WhatsApp support group, regular training sessions, and consistent follow-up. For members who prefer physical interaction, he also runs in-person training sessions at his offices in Benin City, Ogbomoso, and Ede. Wherever you are, he makes sure you have the support you need to actually progress and build a real income.

Can I join and build FHG from my city?

Absolutely. Coach Oluwatosin has active offices in Benin City, Ogbomoso, Ede, and other states, so if you want to come in physically and build from a specific location there is opportunity to do that. For those who prefer to work remotely, mentorship is also fully available online via WhatsApp and phone. Simply reach out to discuss and he will connect you to the right support structure in your city or state.

How long before I start earning?

Most members that Coach Oluwatosin mentors begin seeing their first results within 30 to 90 days, depending on consistency and how quickly they apply the training. The mentorship gives you a clear, practical action plan from day one. The more consistent you are with building your team, the faster you will see your income grow.

Is FHG a legitimate, registered organisation?

Yes. FHG Org Limited is a registered organisation in Nigeria that has been operating since 2015. With over 50,000 members nationwide, a fully transparent structure, and free entry with no hidden charges, FHG has a strong and verifiable track record. Coach Oluwatosin has been an active and successful part of this organisation for years and stands fully behind its legitimacy.
